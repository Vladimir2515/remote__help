Why is it so hard to find relevant remote job?

During a pandemic people trying to leave their homes as little as possible and are looking for ways to earn money, freelancing is more relevant than ever.

But freelancing has its drawbacks: irregular pay, difficulties in finding clients and difficulty in honing professional skills without supervision and training from higher-level specialists.
